For best results, replace this with a PNG image of a Christmas gift!

The app will use gift.png if available, otherwise it will fall back to:
1. gift.svg (included)
2. A programmatically drawn gift box

Recommended image specs:
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Size: 200x200 pixels or larger
- Design: Christmas gift with bow/ribbon
- Style: Bright, festive colors

You can find free Christmas gift images at:
- https://www.flaticon.com/
- https://www.freepik.com/
- https://pixabay.com/

Simply save your image as "gift.png" in this directory!
